About Me

I am a master student in Tsinghua University. I earned my bachelor’s degree from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2023.

My research interest focuses on Machine Learning and Computer Vision. From 2021, I started my research on efficient physiological time series analysis based on knowledge distillation. Now, I focus on efficient video computer vision systems with video motion estimation.

Feel free to contact me by email if you are interest in discussion or collarboration with me.
